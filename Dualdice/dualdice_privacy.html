DualDice - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 12, 2025

D4D Studio (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in the DualDice™ mobile game and related website.

1. Information We Collect

    Personal Information: DualDice does not require users to create an account or provide personal information to play. However, if you contact us (for support or feedback), your email and message will be used solely to respond to your request.

    Non-Personal Information: We may collect anonymized data such as device model, OS version, gameplay time, crash logs, and in-game interactions to improve performance and stability.

    Advertising Data: The game uses Google AdMob to display banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads. AdMob may collect certain device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID) and usage data to deliver ads. Users may opt out of personalized ads through their device settings.

    In-App Purchases: Any future in-app purchases are handled securely via Google Play or the App Store. D4D Studio does not access or store credit card or payment data.

2. How We Use Your Information

    To maintain and improve the app’s functionality and performance.

    To deliver relevant (personalized or non-personalized) advertisements.

    To fix bugs, enhance gameplay, and improve user experience.

    To provide customer support when contacted directly.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. However, third-party services (like AdMob or analytics providers) may process limited, anonymized data as described in their own privacy policies.

4. Analytics & Advertising

DualDice uses third-party SDKs and analytics tools, including:

  • Google AdMob: For showing ads (banner, interstitial, rewarded). AdMob may use device identifiers and cookies for ad personalization. You can learn more here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads
  • Unity Analytics or Firebase: Used to track general app performance, crashes, and anonymous usage data to improve gameplay quality.

5. Children’s Privacy

DualDice is suitable for general audiences and not directed toward children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

6. Your Choices

You can disable personalized ads by changing your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → “Opt out of Ads Personalization”
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising → “Limit Ad Tracking”

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Effective Date” above indicates the most recent revision. Please review it periodically for any changes.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, contact us:

  • Website: DualDice
